Battalion Overview

The 56th Siege Battalion combines tactical adaptability, heavy firepower, and support excellence. Its divisions are designed to secure victory across ground, air, infil, and combat support.

“When the siege begins, the 56th endures until every objective is secured.”

Main Colors 56th Red, 56th Black, and 56th Gray form the battalion's visual identity across armor, banners, and command displays.
Structure 7 divisions, specialized command ranks, and active event-based promotion requirements keep the battalion battle-ready.
Command Central leadership flows from General to Clone Cadet, with promotions based on event participation, training, and votes.

Division Roster

@21st Griffon Division

Led by Alpha ARC Apex

All-around combat force designed to fill gaps on the field. The 21st Griffon is versatile and can deploy nearly any trooper type where they are needed most.

Specialty:
  • Universal deployment
  • Gap filling in combat
  • Support for all trooper types
  • Clone information classes define exceptions

@39th Hydra Division

Led by Alpha ARC Havoc

Heavy assault and demolition experts. The 39th Hydra brings lethal power with explosives, flame, and elite heavy units to dominate the battlefield.

Elite units include:
  • ARC soldiers
  • Flametroopers
  • Demolitionists
  • Marines and Heavy troopers

@89th Wyvern Division

Led by Pilots Maverick and Goose

Air and vehicle support wing that controls the skies and launches rapid reinforcements. They keep ground forces supplied and strike from above.

Elite units include:
  • Jet troopers
  • Vehicle pilots
  • Clone gunners
  • Paratroopers

@95th Yokai Division

Led by Ghillie Trooper "Phantom"

Stealth specialists focused on covert operations, infiltration, and silent takedowns. The 95th operates behind enemy lines with precision and secrecy.

Elite units include:
  • Clone Commandos
  • Assassin Troopers
  • Spec Ops units
  • Blazetroopers

@108th Golem Division

Led by Alpha ARC Odin

Specialist support division that protects and heals allied forces. The 108th ensures combat sustainability with medics and tactical reinforcements.

Elite units include:
  • EVO Troopers
  • Medics
  • Riot Troopers
  • Warriors and ARF troopers

@365th Phoenix Division

Led by Desperado Trooper "Flash"

Recon and scouting unit that maps enemy movement and secures ground. The 365th is prepared for land, sea, and reconnaissance operations.

Elite units include:
  • ARF troopers
  • BARC riders
  • Ghillies
  • AT-RT drivers and Scuba troopers

@580th Mimic Division

No single leader

A coalition of custom and pre-established Star Wars groups finding shelter in the 56th. This division brings outside specialists and allied contingents into the battalion.

Groups include:
  • 90th Odins Blessed
  • Locks Horn Company
  • 88th Serpent Unit
  • Shattered 104th

Rank Requirements

@[GEN] General

1st in command of the battalion.

@[MARC] Marshal Commander

2nd in command of the battalion. Marshal Commanders can only be voted in by the General.

@[COL] Colonel

3rd in command of the battalion. The Colonel can only be voted in by the Marshal Commander and General.

@[LTCOL] Lieutenant Colonel

4th in command of the battalion. The Lieutenant Colonel can only be voted in by the Colonel, Marshal Commander and General.

@[MAJ] Major

5th in command of the battalion. Major can only be voted in by the Lieutenant Colonel, Colonel, Marshal Commander and General.

@[CPT] Captain

Attend 50 Battalion events and host 40 Battalion events (combat and recruiting).

@[1LT] 1st Lieutenant

Host 30 Battalion events (combat and recruiting).

@[2LT] 2nd Lieutenant

Host 20 Battalion events (combat and recruiting).

@[Chief Warrant Officer]

Host 15 Battalion events (combat and recruiting) or co-host 17 events.

@[WO] Warrant Officer

Host 10 battalion events (combat and recruiting) or co-host 12 events.

@[OF] Officer

Must be a current NCO and pass the Officer challenge moderated by a High Comm. Passing allows the rank up.

@[SGM] Sergeant Major

Have an accepted NCO application. Can be promoted from Master Sergeant in nco-application.

@[MSGT] Master Sergeant

Attend 40 battalion events, attend 3 joint events, and be trained into one Phase 3 classification.

@[SFC] Sergeant First Class

Attend 30 Battalion events and attend 2 joint events.

@[SSGT] Staff Sergeant

Attend 25 battalion events and attend 1 joint event.

@[SGT] Sergeant

Attend 20 battalion events and be trained into one Phase 2 classification.

@[CPL] Corporal

Attend 15 Battalion events.

@[LCPL] Lance Corporal

Attend 10 Battalion events and be trained into one Phase 1 classification.

@[PFC] Private First Class

Attend 5 Battalion events.

@[PVT] Private

Complete bootcamp and attend 1 battalion event.

@[CDT] Clone Cadet

Be recruited into the 56th.

Events do not reset. If you attend 5 division events as PFC and get promoted, you only need to attend 5 more events to rank up to LCPL.
