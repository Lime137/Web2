Rank Requirements

Ready to locate your next promotion.

@[GEN] General

1st in command of the battalion.

@[MARC] Marshal Commander

2nd in command of the battalion. Marshal Commanders can only be voted in by the General.

@[COL] Colonel

3rd in command of the battalion. The Colonel can only be voted in by the Marshal Commander and General.

@[LTCOL] Lieutenant Colonel

4th in command of the battalion. The Lieutenant Colonel can only be voted in by the Colonel, Marshal Commander and General.

@[MAJ] Major

5th in command of the battalion. Major can only be voted in by the Lieutenant Colonel, Colonel, Marshal Commander and General.

@[CPT] Captain

Attend 50 Battalion events and host 40 Battalion events (combat and recruiting).

@[1LT] 1st Lieutenant

Host 30 Battalion events (combat and recruiting).

@[2LT] 2nd Lieutenant

Host 20 Battalion events (combat and recruiting).

@[Chief Warrant Officer]

Host 15 Battalion events (combat and recruiting) or co-host 17 events.

@[WO] Warrant Officer

Host 10 battalion events (combat and recruiting) or co-host 12 events.

@[OF] Officer

Must be a current NCO and pass the Officer challenge moderated by a High Comm. Passing allows the rank up.

@[SGM] Sergeant Major

Have an accepted NCO application. Can be promoted from Master Sergeant in nco-application.

@[MSGT] Master Sergeant

Attend 40 battalion events, attend 3 joint events, and be trained into one Phase 3 classification.

@[SFC] Sergeant First Class

Attend 30 Battalion events and attend 2 joint events.

@[SSGT] Staff Sergeant

Attend 25 battalion events and attend 1 joint event.

@[SGT] Sergeant

Attend 20 battalion events and be trained into one Phase 2 classification.

@[CPL] Corporal

Attend 15 Battalion events.

@[LCPL] Lance Corporal

Attend 10 Battalion events and be trained into one Phase 1 classification.

@[PFC] Private First Class

Attend 5 Battalion events.

@[PVT] Private

Complete bootcamp and attend 1 battalion event.

@[CDT] Clone Cadet

Be recruited into the 56th.

Events do not reset. If you attend 5 division events as PFC and get promoted, you only need to attend 5 more events to rank up to LCPL.
