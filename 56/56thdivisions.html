580th Mimic Division

No single leader

The Mimic division is the most flexible 56th entry. It allows allied groups to join the battalion, earn rank, and operate with less strict 56th classification requirements.

Division notes:
  • No 56th custom classes allowed
  • Members can earn rank inside the 56th
  • Structure follows the original allied group classes
  • Designed for temporary allies and supporting groups
